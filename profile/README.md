XDeNovo (纽肽生物) develops AI-driven peptide design solutions for therapeutic and applied
biotechnology. We connect sequence generation, structure prediction, computational evaluation,
and design–test–optimize cycles to help teams move from biological targets to testable peptide
candidates.

## What We Work On

- **De novo peptide design** — discovering and optimizing peptide candidates for challenging
  biological targets.
- **Computational evaluation** — combining structural modeling, docking, scoring, and candidate
  ranking in reproducible workflows.
- **Translational collaboration** — working with research and development teams on therapeutic
  peptides, targeting heads, antimicrobial peptides, and other functional peptide programs.
